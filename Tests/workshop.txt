description=[h2]Features[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*]Display custom location names when entering zones
description=[*]Create zombie-free areas
description=[*]Block Bandits (requires Bandits 2)
description=[*]Prevent safehouse creation
description=[*]Restrict picking up or placing objects
description=[*]Restrict dismantling and crafting
description=[*]Block construction
description=[*]Prevent fire spread
description=[*]Block player entry to specific zones
description=[*]Zone detection works inside [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3543229299]Project RV Interior[/url]
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Built for Extendability[/h2]
description=Other mods can register their own properties directly into the zone editor. Most Phun mods already integrate seamlessly.
description=
description=[h2]What’s New in PhunZones 2[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*]No external dependencies (PhunLib no longer required)
description=[*]Build 42+ focused (B41 support retired)
description=[*]Major performance improvements
description=[*]Completely redesigned zone editor UI
description=[*]True inheritance system — define base zones (Easy / Medium / Hard, etc.) and let other zones inherit their settings. Change it once, update everywhere.
description=[/list]
