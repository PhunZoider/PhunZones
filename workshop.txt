title=PhunZones

description=B41 / B42 server-side mod for customising game behaviour based on location.

description=Define rules, restrictions, and behaviours by zone — from entire cities down to a single building.

description=[H1]TL;DR[/H1]
description=PhunZones lets you control how the game behaves based on where players are.
description=
description=Create zones with custom rules for PvP, zombies, building, looting, fire spread, safehouses, and more.
description=
description=Designed for multiplayer servers, performance-focused, and built to be extended by other mods.

description=
description=[H1]Key Features[/H1]
description=[list]
description=[*]Create and manage zones with unique, configurable properties
description=[list]
description=[*]Optional area title displayed when players enter a zone
description=[*]Toggle zombie spawning
description=[*]Toggle safehouse creation
description=[*]Toggle pickup and placement of objects
description=[*]Toggle scrapping / disassembling
description=[*]Toggle construction
description=[*]Toggle fire spread (brief animation may still play)
description=[*]Prevent players from entering specific zones
description=[*]Toggle bandit spawning (Bandits mod support)
description=[*]Editable default zone to define global behaviour
description=[*]…and more
description=[/list]
description=[/list]

description=
description=[H2]PvP Behaviour (B41 vs B42)[/H2]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]Build 41:[/b] PvP can be enabled or disabled per zone (unless disabled globally in server INI)
description=[*][b]Build 42:[/b] Native PvP zones are handled by the base game and cannot be overridden
description=[/list]
description=
description=As a result, PvP toggling inside PhunZones is deprecated in B42 but retained for B41 compatibility.

description=
description=[H2]Built to Be Extended[/H2]
description=PhunZones is designed as both a standalone system and a foundation for other mods.
description=
description=Other mods can query zone data or hook into PhunZones behaviour to drive their own logic.
description=
description=This makes it ideal for:
description=[list]
description=[*]Custom loot systems
description=[*]Faction or territory mechanics
description=[*]Event-driven gameplay
description=[*]Server-specific rulesets
description=[/list]

description=
description=The default zone can be edited to define global behaviour — ideal for mods extending PhunZones functionality
description=(e.g. PhunLewts).

description=
description=[H2]Player-Friendly Feedback[/H2]
description=[list]
description=[*]Clean UI widget showing the current area name (e.g. Louisville – Mall)
description=[*]Helps players understand why behaviour changes between locations
description=[*]Reduces confusion without immersion-breaking messages
description=[/list]
